About Our Service

Extreme Appliance Cleaning provides professional deep cleaning services for all your home appliances in Nicosia, Cyprus. Our certified technicians use eco-friendly products and specialized equipment to remove dirt, grease, and bacteria, extending the life of your appliances and improving their performance.

Our Cleaning Process

We follow a meticulous 4-step process to ensure your appliances are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized:

1
Inspection
We assess the condition of your appliance and identify problem areas.
2
Deep Cleaning
Using specialized tools and eco-friendly cleaners to remove all dirt and grime.
3
Sanitization
Killing 99.9% of bacteria and germs for a hygienic clean.
4
Final Check
Ensuring everything is spotless and functioning perfectly.
